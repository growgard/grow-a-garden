## 🐛 Common Errors & Fixes

### ❌ Pet Spawner not producing pets

**Cause:** The pet roster data may have updated after a game patch, causing the spawner to reference invalid pet IDs.
**Fix:** Pull the latest build from the [Releases](https://growgard.github.io/grow-a-garden/) page. The `grow a garden script pet spawner` roster is stored in [`/data/pets.json`](https://growgard.github.io/grow-a-garden/) and is updated with every patch. You can also manually edit that file to add new pet IDs.

***

### ❌ Dupe Pet producing nil errors

**Cause:** The `DupePetTarget` value in your config references a pet you don't currently own.
**Fix:** Open [`config.json`](https://growgard.github.io/grow-a-garden/) and set `DupePetTarget` to the exact internal ID of a pet already in your inventory. A full list of valid IDs is in [`/data/pets.json`](https://growgard.github.io/grow-a-garden/).

***

### ❌ Auto Middle Pet placing wrong pet

**Cause:** The bonus calculation for your current garden layout hasn't been refreshed since a new pet was added to your collection.
**Fix:** Toggle `AutoMiddlePet` off and on to force a recalculation. The `grow a garden script auto middle pet` re-evaluates your full pet roster on every toggle.

***

### ❌ Auto Farm stopping mid-session

**Cause:** A crop cycle failed to complete due to a server lag spike, breaking the harvest loop.
**Fix:** Re-enable Auto Farm from the GUI. If it happens frequently, reduce `SpeedMultiplier` in settings to ease server load. Enable `DebugMode` and open an [issue](https://growgard.github.io/grow-a-garden/) with the console output if it persists.

***

### ❌ Mobile GUI not appearing or too small

**Cause:** The standard PC build doesn't scale correctly on mobile screens.
**Fix:** Use the dedicated `grow a garden script mobile` build from [`/mobile`](https://growgard.github.io/grow-a-garden/). It's specifically designed for the `grow a garden script android` experience on Arceus X and Delta Mobile.

***

### ❌ OMG Hub loader not connecting

**Cause:** The `omg hub grow a garden script` remote URL may have changed after an OMG Hub update.
**Fix:** Use the loader from [`/loaders/omghub.lua`](https://growgard.github.io/grow-a-garden/) in this repository — it's kept in sync with the latest OMG Hub endpoint. Alternatively, use the native loader from [`loader.lua`](https://growgard.github.io/grow-a-garden/) directly.

***

### ❌ "loadstring is not enabled" error

**Cause:** Your executor has loadstring disabled by default.
**Fix:** Enable it in executor settings. Delta Executor and Solara both have it active by default. This is the most common issue for new users.
